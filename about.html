Crook District

The band was formed in the 2010´s by five friends from a small town close to Amsterdam. Because all band members we're living on the same street/district, the band decided to pay hommage to their origins by (poorly) translating the district's name to a band name. After several years of playing in their late teen's and early twenty's the band lost touch with the music and eachother for a couple of years, only to return back to writing songs in 2018. The result of the latest songwriting process can be heard on the Cinco Más EP. The band continue to play, write and record their own music from both God's Window Studios and Cinco Más Studios.
